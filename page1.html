Destiny 2 - Basic knowledge

Destiny 2 is a first person shooter. The game is on PS4 XBOX One and PC. The base game is free as of oct 1st 2019, however there are DLC that are very important if you plan on activly playing the game. There is also cross save, meaning that you can use the same account across plaforms.
The main appeal of the game is the looter part of it. This means that the game is full of diffrent types of loot, and on top of the random loot that you will be getting there is something called weapon and armor perks. These perks are also random. an example of a perk on a weapon would be rangefinder, when you aim down sights you have more range. There are hundreds of diffrent perks, and thousands of diffrent weapons. if you are after a specific weapon with some specific perks it could take hundreds of hours to finnaly get, but the feeling of getting it is one of the things that keep people playing the game.
The communtiy for the game is another big part of why it is fun.

Activites


There are lots of diffrent activities in destiny. One of the main things that an average player will do once they get the game is the first few story missions. The story missions are not ment to be very hard but they are ment to get you into the game. Some of the harder activities include nightfalls raids and heroic menagerie. Of corse that is just the pve (player vs enemy) activites. There are several pvp (player vs player) activites in the game. Quickplay and competitive are the main pvp activites but iron banner also comes every once in a while. First I will tell you about crucible.

Crucible


Crucible is the pvp mode in destiny. it is divided up into quickplay, rumble, a weekly gamemode, and competitve. There is also private matches where you can make a match for you and your friends to play. Quickplay is a mix of clash, where the team with the most kils wins. As well as controll, where you have to capture zones then kill the enemies. Rumble is a 6 person free for all match. The random weekly gamemode could be pretty much anything, so we will only talk about one of the weekly game modes, iron banner. Iron banner is controll with a few special things about it. one of them is that your gear level acctualy matters there. the other is that if your team can capture every zone at once they will be locked for 30 seconds giving your team a huge advangtage. Competitive is where your wins and losses acctualy matter. The more wins you get the more your rank goes up, and the more losses you get the more your rank goes down. This has the same game modes as quickplay with the addition of countdown and survival. survival is clash but with 6 lives per team, first team to run ut of lives and be wiped loses. Countdown is a mode where you plant and defuse a bomb. competitive is 4v4 while quickplay and iron banner are 6v6.

Classes

There are three diffrent classes of characters in destiny 2. There are Hunters Warlocks and Titans. The main diffrence between the three are their abilities, and how they jump. They also each wear diffrent armor. Each class has three subclasses. solar, arc, and void. Each subclass has three trees inside it.

Hunters

Hunters have a standard doubble or tripple jump as their jump. Their solar subclass is golden gun. the top tree has normal throwing knives and you can shoot the golden gun up to 6 times, returning a bullet with each kill. The bottom tree has three bullets that can deal critical damage, and you also have explosive throwing knives. The middle tree is blade birrage, where you will vault into the air and throw explosive knives everywhere. you mele ability is throwing burnning knives. Hunters void subclass is tether. the top tree has a smoke bomb that does damage, and they can shoot a single bow shot (a tether) that will disorient enemys and cause them to share damage as well as take more damage. bottome tree has smoke that makes you invisible. you can shoot multiple thethers but they dont do as much damage or last as long. Middle tree is called spectral blades, and you get a pair of knives and can slash them around. You also get a damaging smoke bomb withe middle tree. The arc subclass for hunter is arcstrider. your mele ability will do one of three things, 1. disorient enemies 2. do more damage after a kill. 3. do an upercut. The super is fairly simple where you just get a big staff are run arround swinging it at whatever you need to. the middle tree gives you the ability to deflect any projectile with it.

Of corse there are Warlocks and Titans, however I am not very experinced on them so I will not talked about them here.

Rarities

There are three diffrent rarities that you will commonly see in the game, rare, legendary, and exotic. There are also common and uncommon but you stop seeing them once you have made progress in the game. Legendary is the rarity that matters most in the endgame alongside exotic. exotics are the rarest and can be seperated by their yellow color. You can only have one exotic weapon and one exotic armor on at once.

Raids

Raids are the endgame activity for pve. THey are six person activities that usualy take an hour or two. They are usualy very complicated and take a good team to complete. They involve anything from boss fights to puzzles. They even reward some of the best gear in the game.

There are of corse many other things about the game but these are just the basics.
